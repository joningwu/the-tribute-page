Steve Jobs

The chairman, chief executive officer (CEO), and co-founder of Apple Inc.

Steve Jobs is showing an iPhone

Timeline

  • 1954 - Born in San Francisco
  • 1968 - Was given a summer job by Bill Hewlett
  • 1972 - Enrolled at Reed College in Portland, Oregon but drop out after one year
  • 1976 - Founded Apple Computer Company with Wozniak, and Ronald Wayne in Jobs's parents' Crist Drive home
  • 1985 - After the Macintosh's failure to defeat the IBM PC, Jobs left the Apple board and founded NeXT Inc.
  • 1997 - With NeXT was acquired by Apple Inc., Jobs was back to the company he had cofounded
  • 2003 - Was diagnosed with cancer
  • 2011 - Died at his Palo Alto, California home
